Creating an account on nostr is an act of responsibility.

Create your account, save your 12 secret words and start interacting with the nostr protocol.

Decentralization brings with it the responsibility to securely store your login credentials (12 words or private key). If you lose them you will not be able to recover your account.

12 secret words? What the heck is that?!

Well, imagine you're at a super exclusive party, only for your closest friends. Now, the only way to get in is through a password, but this time it's not something typical like a combination of numbers and letters. Instead, they give you a list of 12 words, which are super special because together they are the key to get into the party.

Here's how it works at Nostros. When you sign up, you create a set of 12 words that are called a "seed phrase" and are the only way to access your account. Think of it as a master key that unlocks your Nostros profile.

Why 12 words and not something longer? Well, it's about making it easier for you to remember and write the seed phrase. Plus, it's more secure than using a simple password that someone else could guess.

So, if you want to join the party at Nostros, make sure you write down those 12 secret words in a safe place, don't share them with anyone and have fun connecting with people from all over the world!
